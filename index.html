Founder

Who founded Alpenliebe? After tasting success and popularity in the European markets, Perfetti marched towards India in 1994. It merged with Van Melle in 2002 to become Perfetti Van Melle (PVM). PVMI is credited by the brand custodian to create the Indian caramelized candy segment with Alpenliebe, launched in 1995.
